# OBS-006 — Retry Efficiency Monitoring

## Objective

Evaluate whether retry mechanisms are effective in recovering from failures.

## What to observe

- retry success rate
- retry volume vs success
- repeated retries without resolution

## What it reveals

- ineffective retry logic
- systemic failure conditions
- degraded dependencies

## Expected invariants

- retries should improve success rate
- retries should not cause instability

## Failure signals

- low retry success rate
- retry storms
- repeated failures

## Related risks

- Architecture / lack of idempotency enforcement across retries
- Architecture / ambiguity of state ownership during retry execution
- Architecture / absence of coordinated retry strategy across components
- Architecture / amplification of failure due to uncontrolled retry behavior
- Reliability / retry misbehavior
- Integration / dependency instability
- Operational resilience / recovery inefficiency

## Notes

Retries should converge, not amplify failure.
